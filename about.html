Through these experiences, I have developed and honed my skills in C++, MySQL, Microsoft PowerPoint, leadership, and communication. I am always eager to take on new challenges and opportunities that allow me to grow and collaborate with others. My goal is to become a proficient and impactful coder who can make a positive difference in the world.
